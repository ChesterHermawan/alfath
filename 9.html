Liuchengqi (Alfath) is a Jakarta-based designer and videographer working in branding and identity, videography, and media. His work moves between responses to human joy and suffering, dealing with topics such as human displacement and workers’ rights. He reflects on how his position as a first-generation that go to private college informed his work, and aims to utilize design and personal experience to visualize emotion and promote social justice.

Alfath currently pursuing a visual communication design and a minor in Digital Transformation in Binus University.

He currently works freelance as a graphic designer. Alfath loves Toy Figures, Roblox, renaissance art, clothing, and cats.
