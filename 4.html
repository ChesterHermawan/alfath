The UNFINISHED PRJCT. concept is a groundbreaking cocktail bar in Jakarta, Indonesia, rooted in circularity and sustainability. This concept deck outlines a vision for a cocktail experience that transforms discarded ingredients into inventive drinks, promoting a circular approach to resource use in hospitality. UNFINISHED PRJCT. seeks to reduce, reuse, and recycle by sourcing wasted ingredients from local suppliers, maximizing their use across cocktails, and minimizing unnecessary waste.
